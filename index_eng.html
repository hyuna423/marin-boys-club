INSPIRATIONS

Marin Boys Club
We wanted to take a long-term and systemic movement against pollution of the marine environment. Marine Boys Club is a gathering of people who love and care for the sea and carry out activities to protect the sea environment. We introduce various undersea animals, develop affection for each animal, express oneself by avatarizing the undersea animal that matches the personality of the animal, and support environmental protection activities appropriate to it. The elements and patterns of all undersea animals were produced according to the characteristics and inclinations of each animal.
